EXAMPLES OF COMPUTER VIRUS

POISONLVY (2005)

Poisonlvy is a computer security nightmare!, it allows the attacker to control the infected user's computer secretly.

Poisonlvy is a Malware and this type of malware is usually called remote access trojan, because it provides maximum control to the penetrator through a backdoor. Once the virus is installed on it the penetrator can have full access to control the targeted computer.

Poisonlvy has been used in sophisticated attacks against dozens of European and American firms, including in defense and chemical industries.
